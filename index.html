Policies

Booking Policy

If not arranged otherwise, check in time is 16:00 and check out time is 11:00

Minimum booking is 2 nights. For bookings of less what 7 nights the final cleaning fee (CHF 500) is added. Bookings of 7 nights or more include final cleaning fee.

A CHF 1,000 insurance deposit is required. Access to the apartment will not be granted without this deposit. Absolutely no smoking inside the apartment! This will result in loss of deposit. A damage check will be performed after departure, and the deposit will be returned within 2 weeks of the departure date.

Pets might be allowed. Please, contact to discuss.

Cancellation Policy

For a 50% refund, cancellation must be made 30 full days prior to local (Swiss) check in time (or by 16:00 if not specified) on the day of check in. If the guest cancels less than 30 days in advance, the nights not spent are not refunded. If guest arrives and decides to leave early, the nights not spent are not refunded.
